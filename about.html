OUR MISSION

Empowering People

The demonetization of November 2016 was arguably the major catalyst in digitizing India. The estimated value of mobile wallet transactions in 2020 across India was 36.5 trillion Indian rupees. This was expected to more than triple by 2024. Rising smartphone penetration along with increasing competition in the domestic market could be attributed to the growing value. The value of card payments in India for the fiscal year 2020 amounted to approximately 15 trillion Indian rupees. This was after a consistent increase in card payments since fiscal year 2014, as a result of the government's initiative to digitize the country, catalyzed by the demonetization of currency notes in November 2016. Despite all these milestone achievements Cash is still king in India. Our mission is to empower people toward digital transactions thereby the idea of digital india can come to reality.

Building Trust

With the adoption of technology, there has been a significant rise in digital payments in India. At the same time, the threat landscape has mushroomed too. Nearly half of Indian consumers(47%) are more worried about frauds while making digital transactions amid the coronavirus crisis, shows a study. About 28 per cent of consumers are now exercising more caution while making payments using various digital modes, according to a study conducted by YouGov and ACI Worldwide, a real-time electronic payment and banking solutions provider. Our dedicated service quality team and highly qualified executive have developed models that make sure our client will get whatever promised. Our hand-holding approach would also guide you step by step to build your trust toward digital transactions. Therefore come forward with us and let's contribute toward the country's GDP together by following the digital India campaign.
